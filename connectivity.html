Inter-DC Traffic Engineering

Traffic between datacenters is engineered to optimize performance, reliability, and cost:

  • Critical Applications: Dark Fiber with MPLS Backup
  • Bulk Data Transfer: Dark Fiber with QoS
  • Management Traffic: Out-of-Band Management Network
  • Replication Traffic: Dedicated VLAN with QoS
